My Role In The Company

I’ve had the pleasure of beginning my career at this company in 2019 as an Internal Service Advisor at one of Ourisman’s Kia dealerships. In this position, I coordinate all internal repairs within the dealership for both used and new cars in our inventory. With over 300 cars in our inventory, there’s always a high demand for repair and maintenance orders. Writing up various service tickets and dispatching the work to technicians is one of my many responsibilities as an Internal Service Advisor. I establish and maintain a good rapport with the many vendors that collaborate with our dealership. Working closely with the sales team, I oversee which repair orders or appearance packages are to be approved by the general manager. As I follow up with customers and update them on the status of their cars, I ensure that they are satisfied with the final outcome of their purchase.

The Ourisman Automotive Group was established back in the 1920's and has grown to be one of the largest auto dealers in the country today.
