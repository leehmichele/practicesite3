Performance with Plastic Nancy

Slash Run, DC

"Filling In"

In 2019, I had the pleasure of filling in for a band called Plastic Nancy as Sam and Brian couldn’t make it to the performance at Slash Run. I was given the opportunity by my good friend Mikey who is a member of the band. Although Matt (the drummer who filled in) and I had learned the set two hours before the show, it was a fun and successful performance nonetheless.

Los Sadicos

Bossa Lounge, DC

"Lead guitarist for Los Sadicos"

From 2017-2018, I’ve had the privilege of playing for a tribute band called Los Sadicos. We covered songs by a Peruvian rock band from the 60’s and performed in several small venues such as Chapala Blue Beetle Rock Bar, Bossa Lounge, and Marx Café.
